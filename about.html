About

Inspired from the vibrant streets of Berlin, I am a passionate tattoo artist renowned for my love for in fine-line tattoos. With an artistry deeply rooted in the city's eclectic culture, I am trying to bring a unique touch to the world of body art. My fascination to make each individual even more themselves and underline who they are by making them feel the best in their own skin. And why? Because you deserve to love yourself and create the version you want to be the most from the in- and outside. Each stroke is carefully crafted, creating designs that are both elegant and timeless. From minimalist designs to intricate patterns, my work reflects my commitment to precision and artistic expression. If you are interested in my art, please reach out to me. At the moment I am working at "Inklove studio Berlin". You can contact me via this page, the studio or instagram. I am looking forward to meet you! Love, Susi

Mission and vision

The mission and vision I have is to tell a story with each and every tattoo. I am using ink for all skin colours, the ink is high quality that will make your skin thank you and last longer for years without any signs of damage that may require redesigning over the years.
